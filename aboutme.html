Meet Ashley, a hands-on UX/UI Designer
with a knack for storytelling and collaboration.

Ash, for short.

As a creative, she’s well-versed in branding, visual, and interaction design. Driven by a deep curiosity for storytelling and collaboration, she strives to elevate user experience through memorable, yet minimal interfaces that simplify usage and promote accessibility.

To her, creating unique experiences starts with understanding the heart of an idea or goal from start to finish, truly keeping in mind the needs of the audience.

She’s a dedicated Laker fan, tech & fitness enthusiast, proud gamer, and matcha lover.

Aside from designing, you can find her hiking and traveling with family and friends. Oh and of course, alongside her German Shepherd Husky pup, Jemma.
